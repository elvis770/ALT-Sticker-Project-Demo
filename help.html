Activity Level Suggestions

Low Activity Level

  1. Encourage gentle movement like stretching or short walks.
  2. Motivate the patient with supportive conversations.
  3. Check for signs of tiredness or weakness and report concerns.
  4. Engage them in simple tasks like coloring or puzzles.

Good Activity Level

  1. Encourage continuing their current routine.
  2. Praise their efforts to keep them motivated.
  3. Adjust activities if they are too easy or too hard.
  4. Ensure proper hydration and nutrition.

High Activity Level

  1. Watch for signs of overexertion like fast breathing or heartbeat.
  2. Encourage short breaks and relaxation.
  3. Suggest calming activities like reading or soft music.
  4. Check for signs of agitation or anxiety and stay with them if needed.
